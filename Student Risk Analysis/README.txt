The application should be tested through X-Code on OSX. Open the X-Code project from the ZIP file, hit the play-button icon which will open iOS simulator to run the project. Go to “Hardware”-> “Keyboard” and deselect “Connect Hardware Keyboard”. 

Known limitation: do not try to click on blank space in the UI to get out of textfields. Click on either another textfield, the button, or the picker.
